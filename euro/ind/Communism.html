The Rise of Socialism
  • Thomas More - "First Socialist" based on Values
  • Claude Henri de Saint-Simon | 1760-1825
    • Hated the rich, but liked Industrialist
    • Loved the "Doers" people who went out and did things for society
    • Thinks people are naturally greedy
    • Do away with Inheritance - money when a person dies goes to the government for the Greater Good
  • Charles Fourier | 1772-1837
    • "The first feminist"
    • 1665 people lived in a "Phalanx" where they could do whatever they wanted based on their role
    • Everyone worked at whatever they did
    • Work for 2 hours then rest for 1 hour then repeat.
    • People could move from Phalanx to Phalanx
    • When a man came to a Phalanx they got to choose out of an index of women who they wanted to have sex with.
  • Robert Owen | 1771-1858
    • Utopian Socialist
    • Owned a textile factory in New Lanark, Scotland
    • Set up a model community in New Harmony, Indiana
    • Decreased working hours
    • Nice and cheap housing for his workers
    • He made sure his workers were atleast 10 before joining
    • Kids went to schools in the factory
    • Wanted to create the worlds first National Union
  • Louis Blanc | 1811-1882
    • Practical Socialist
    • Wanted to create workshops where workers had a stake in their factory they worked at
    • Created multiple workshops in Paris
  • Pierre-Joseph Proudhon
    • Anarchist that believes if the government falls the people will behave
  • Karl Marx | 1818-1883
    • Dubbed all the previous Socialists "Utopian"
    • Believed that Money rules the world and knows the people won't give up their money
    • Believed the Rich should be killed
    • Dialectic
      • There is two opposing sides
      • The two sides fight and a new side is created, which then has its own opposition
      • Infinite Cycle
    • Believes History has been about the Haves and Have Nots
    • Proletariat vs Bourgeois
      • End of the Dialectics he believed
    • Stage 1. Dramatic Exploitation of the workers
      • Extraordinary amount of people are being Exploited
    • Stage 2. Bloody and Spontaneous Revolution
      • The workers will rise up and kill their overseers and the rich and Bourgeois 
    • Stage 3. Dictatorship of the Proletariats
      • EXTREMELY Brief amount of time to organize the people and assign them roles
    • Stage 4. Government will Wither
      • Government is no longer needed since everyone will have a role and is treated humanely
    • Communist Manifesto 
      • His written thesis of what should happen and how people should live
    • Resents the Capitalists since they are just profiting off of someone else's labor
